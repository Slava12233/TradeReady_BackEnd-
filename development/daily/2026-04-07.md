### Decisions

- Introduced typed `BattleLiveParticipantSchema` instead of `dict[str, Any]` to enforce the 13-field contract at the schema level — prevents future field-name drift between backend and frontend.
- `sharpe_ratio` and `max_drawdown_pct` are intentionally null during a live battle (rolling Sharpe with few data points would be misleading); they populate only in the results endpoint after completion.

### Issues Found

- Root cause of crash: `!== null` (strict) doesn't guard `undefined`; the field arrived as `undefined` due to name mismatch, not `null`. Loose `!= null` is the correct guard when API fields could be missing entirely.
- The `dict[str, Any]` pattern in schemas gives zero protection against backend/frontend field-name drift. Any live endpoint returning participant data should use a typed schema.
